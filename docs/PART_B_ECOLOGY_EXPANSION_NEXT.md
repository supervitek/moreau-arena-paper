# Part B Ecology Expansion Next

Last updated: 2026-03-20
Status: Approved next-layer spec
Owner: Codex acting as chief engineer

## Purpose

This document captures the next safe content expansion for `Part B` after the first working house-agent loop.

The rule is simple:

- deepen the existing ecology before adding new benchmark surfaces

## Current Safe Expansion Order

1. Deepen `The Arena`
- richer spoils text
- clearer combat aftermath
- stronger return-report language around streaks, defeats, and recovery

2. Deepen `Cave of Catharsis`
- stronger omen language
- clearer push-versus-extract tension
- richer aftermath reporting around depth, value, and injury

3. Enrich the operator fantasy
- better `while away` reports
- cleaner watch status
- more legible priorities and risk language

Only after that:

4. Consider a third zone

## What Counts As Safe Content

Safe additions do not change the benchmark contract:

- flavor text
- product copy
- spoils summaries
- omen summaries
- richer inspect/return cards
- better zone-specific reporting

Unsafe additions require a season boundary:

- new verbs
- new score families
- new zone mechanics that change action outcomes materially
- new hidden state that only house agents can see

## Next Candidate Layers

### Arena Spoils

Add purely descriptive fallout:

- clean win
- ugly win
- expensive loss
- morale swing

This sharpens the world without creating a new mechanic.

### Cave Omens

Add descriptive tension:

- shallow warning
- greed signal
- near-break extraction
- unstable descent

Again, narrative only unless the season contract is intentionally revised.

## Red Line

Do not quietly smuggle a third zone or a new economy into `B5` under the label of "content."
