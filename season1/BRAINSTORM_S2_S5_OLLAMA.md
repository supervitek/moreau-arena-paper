### 1. CONSENSUS POINTS

The council achieved strong alignment (3/3 panelists) on the following pillars:

**Core Geometry & Physics**
- **Hexagonal grid migration** with momentum-based movement mechanics, specifically **ice slide physics** where units continue 2 tiles in the movement direction, creating positioning puzzles.
- **Three-tier elevation system** (whether as distinct levels or full z-axis verticality) with asymmetric movement costs (expensive to climb, free/cheap to descend) and fall damage mechanics.
- **Destructible terrain** that reshapes the arena mid-combat after sustained damage, creating emergent pathways.

**Combat Evolution**
- **Persistent limb-specific damage** that carries between rounds in a series (scar tissue/injury systems), affecting movement speed or attack power based on targeted body parts.
- **Status effect stacking**: Bleeding (damage over time), fatigue (reduced efficiency over long matches), and adrenaline surges (stat boosts when near death).
- **Combo mechanics** rewarding consecutive hits with ultimate abilities or AoE blasts.

**Environmental Design**
- **Dynamic hazards** including proximity mines, poison clouds with wind-based drift, periodic lightning strikes, earthquakes causing random displacement, and rising flood waters.
- **Fog of war** limiting vision to 3 tiles, with mechanics revealing positions (blood trails, noise propagation).
- **Elemental terrain synergies** (e.g., lava damage mitigated by adjacent water sources).

**Meta-Mechanics**
- **Between-round evolution**: Genetic mutation, trait absorption from defeated opponents, or breeding systems occurring *between* matches (not during combat).
- **2v2 team modes** with shared health pools or symbiotic fusion mechanics.
- **Objective modes**: King of the Hill (control central tiles) and Capture the Flag (escort mechanics).
- **Boss encounters**: Periodic raid bosses or Minotaur-style elite creatures interrupting standard play.

**Community & Spectator Ecosystem**
- **AI transparency tools**: Genome decoders revealing AI decision-making processes, heatmap replays showing death zones and chokepoints.
- **Content creation**: Community map editors (with Steam Workshop integration), DNA splicers for custom animal creation via JSON parameters, and modding APIs.
- **Engagement layers**: Prediction markets for wagering, crowd effects influencing combatants, and seasonal championships with prize pools.
