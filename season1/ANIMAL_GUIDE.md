# Season 1 Animal Guide

All 14 animals available in Moreau Arena Season 1. Each animal has a **passive** (always active), two **abilities** (proc on any given tick based on their proc tier), and a unique playstyle.

**Proc tiers:**
- **Standard (4.5%):** Base chance to trigger each tick. Modified by WIL (yours boosts it, opponent's resists it).
- **Strong (3.5%):** Lower base chance, but typically more powerful effects.

---

## 1. Bear

**Role:** Burst

*When backed into a corner, the Bear doesn't retreat -- it rampages.*

**Passive -- Fury Protocol:** When your HP drops below 50%, gain +50% ATK for 3 ticks.

**Ability 1 -- Berserker Rage** (Strong, 3.5%): Gain +60% ATK for 3 ticks, but lose 40% dodge for the duration.

**Ability 2 -- Last Stand** (Strong, 3.5%): Survive a killing blow at 1 HP and retaliate with +100% ATK. Single-use per fight.

**Strategy:** Stack ATK for maximum Fury Protocol damage. Your low-HP zone is your power zone -- the lower you go, the harder you hit. Pair with enough HP to survive reaching that threshold, but don't over-invest in durability or you waste your kit.

---

## 2. Buffalo

**Role:** Tank

*Slow to anger, impossible to move.*

**Passive -- Thick Hide:** The first hit you take deals 50% reduced damage.

**Ability 1 -- Thick Hide Block** (Standard, 4.5%): Block ALL damage from 1 hit entirely.

**Ability 2 -- Iron Will** (Strong, 3.5%): Heal 12% of your max HP. Single-use per fight.

**Strategy:** Stack HP to maximize the value of Iron Will's percentage-based heal. WIL investment pairs well, giving you regen on top of your innate tankiness. You outlast most opponents but beware sustained damage dealers who ignore your front-loaded defenses.

---

## 3. Boar

**Role:** Bruiser

*Lowers its head and doesn't stop.*

**Passive -- Charge:** Your first attack deals +50% damage.

**Ability 1 -- Stampede** (Standard, 4.5%): Gain +50% ATK and your opponent skips their next attack.

**Ability 2 -- Gore** (Strong, 3.5%): Deal an attack at -40% ATK, but it ignores dodge entirely.

**Strategy:** Lead with Charge for a devastating burst opener. Gore punishes dodge-heavy enemies like Fox and Eagle who rely on evasion. Invest in ATK to make your opener and Stampede hits count -- you want fights decided quickly.

---

## 4. Tiger

**Role:** Mobility / Burst

*Patience. Precision. One perfect strike.*

**Passive -- Ambush Wiring:** If your SPD is greater than your opponent's SPD, your first attack deals 2x damage.

**Ability 1 -- Pounce** (Standard, 4.5%): Gain +70% ATK and your opponent skips their next attack.

**Ability 2 -- Hamstring** (Standard, 4.5%): Reduce opponent's SPD by 55% and dodge by 10% for 4 ticks.

**Strategy:** Invest in SPD to guarantee Ambush Wiring triggers -- a 2x opening hit is massive. Hamstring cripples other fast enemies, stripping their speed advantage. You dominate the early fight; close it out before sustain fighters can recover.

---

## 5. Wolf

**Role:** Sustained / Proc

*The pack hunts as one. You carry them all.*

**Passive -- Pack Sense:** All your ability proc rates gain +1.5% (added to base rates).

**Ability 1 -- Pack Howl** (Standard, 4.5%): Gain +30% ATK for 4 ticks.

**Ability 2 -- Rend** (Standard, 4.5%): Apply 5% max HP bleed damage per tick for 3 ticks.

**Strategy:** WIL investment amplifies both your proc rates and regen, making Wolf the sustained damage king. With Pack Sense boosting all your procs to 6%, you trigger abilities more often than anyone. Play the long game and grind opponents down.

---

## 6. Monkey

**Role:** Wildcard

*Chaos is a ladder -- and Monkey climbs fast.*

**Passive -- Primate Cortex:** Copy a random ability from your opponent.

**Ability 1 -- Chaos Strike** (Standard, 4.5%): Deal an attack with a random damage multiplier between 0.8x and 2.2x.

**Ability 2 -- Mimic** (Strong, 3.5%): Copy your opponent's passive at 75% strength.

**Strategy:** High variance -- your power level depends heavily on what you copy. Monkey is strongest against enemies with powerful passives and abilities (Bear's Fury Protocol at 75% is still devastating). Weakest against simple stat-check fighters with underwhelming passives.

---

## 7. Porcupine

**Role:** Anti-Melee

*Hit me. I dare you.*

**Passive -- Quill Armor:** Reflect 25% of melee damage back to the attacker.

**Ability 1 -- Spike Shield** (Standard, 4.5%): Gain +50% reflect damage for 3 ticks (total 75% reflect).

**Ability 2 -- Curl Up** (Strong, 3.5%): Block the next hit entirely, but you are immobile for 2 ticks.

**Strategy:** The ultimate counter for ATK stackers -- Bear and Boar hurt themselves hitting you. Invest in HP to survive while they kill themselves on your quills. Beware DOT and anti-heal, which bypass reflect entirely.

---

## 8. Scorpion

**Role:** Anti-Heal

*No recovery. No mercy. Just venom.*

**Passive -- Venom Gland:** Your attacks reduce the target's healing and regen by 50% for 3 ticks.

**Ability 1 -- Envenom** (Standard, 4.5%): Apply 3% max HP poison DOT for 3 ticks. Refreshes on reapplication, never stacks.

**Ability 2 -- Tail Strike** (Strong, 3.5%): Deal +80% ATK damage, ignoring 50% of the target's damage reduction.

**Strategy:** Hard counter to WIL-heavy regen builds and Buffalo's tankiness. Venom Gland halves all healing, and Tail Strike pierces damage reduction. Struggles against fast burst fighters like Tiger and Eagle who can kill you before your debuffs matter.

---

## 9. Vulture

**Role:** Finisher

*Patient. Circling. Waiting for weakness.*

**Passive -- Carrion Feeder:** Gain +1% ATK for every 10% HP your opponent is missing.

**Ability 1 -- Death Spiral** (Standard, 4.5%): Gain +40% ATK for 2 ticks if opponent is below 40% HP.

**Ability 2 -- Feast** (Strong, 3.5%): Heal 20% of damage dealt this tick.

**Strategy:** You get stronger as fights progress -- a nearly dead opponent gives you up to +10% ATK from Carrion Feeder alone, plus Death Spiral on top. Pair with WIL for sustain to outlast opponents, then finish with massive scaling damage. Avoid burst fighters who kill you before you ramp up.

---

## 10. Rhino

**Role:** Anti-Burst Tank

*You can hit hard. But you can't hit hard enough.*

**Passive -- Bulwark Frame:** Any single hit exceeding 18% of your max HP is reduced by 35%.

**Ability 1 -- Horn Charge** (Standard, 4.5%): Deal +60% ATK damage.

**Ability 2 -- Fortify** (Strong, 3.5%): Gain +30% damage reduction for 4 ticks.

**Strategy:** Stack HP to maximize the Bulwark threshold -- the higher your max HP, the bigger the hit needs to be before reduction kicks in. Hard counter to Bear and Tiger burst. Weak against sustained DOT damage that comes in small ticks, bypassing Bulwark entirely.

---

## 11. Viper

**Role:** DOT

*A slow death is still a death.*

**Passive -- Hemotoxin:** Your attacks apply 2% max HP DOT for 3 ticks. Refreshes on reapplication, never stacks -- just resets the timer.

**Ability 1 -- Toxic Bite** (Standard, 4.5%): Refresh DOT and extend duration to 5 ticks.

**Ability 2 -- Shed Skin** (Strong, 3.5%): Remove all your debuffs and heal 8% max HP.

**Strategy:** Consistent ticking damage that wears down tanks over time. Your DOT bypasses Rhino's Bulwark Frame since it deals small per-tick damage. Countered hard by Scorpion's anti-heal, which cuts your DOT healing and sustain in half.

---

## 12. Fox

**Role:** Anti-Proc / Evasion

*You can't hit what you can't predict.*

**Passive -- Cunning:** Your opponent's proc rates are reduced by 30%.

**Ability 1 -- Outfox** (Standard, 4.5%): Dodge the next 2 attacks entirely.

**Ability 2 -- Trick** (Strong, 3.5%): Steal your opponent's next proc -- you get the effect instead of them.

**Strategy:** Shuts down proc-reliant fighters like Wolf and Monkey. High SPD combined with Cunning makes you incredibly evasive and disruptive. Struggles against raw ATK fighters like Bear and Boar who don't need procs to deal damage.

---

## 13. Eagle

**Role:** Kite / Harass

*You fight on the ground. Eagle fights from the sky.*

**Passive -- Aerial Advantage:** While your HP is above 60%, gain +15% dodge and +1 movement range.

**Ability 1 -- Dive** (Strong, 3.5%): Deal +100% ATK in a single devastating strike, but lose Aerial Advantage for 5 ticks.

**Ability 2 -- Tailwind** (Standard, 4.5%): Gain +10% dodge for 3 ticks.

**Strategy:** Kite with superior movement and build SPD for maximum evasion. Dive is high-risk, high-reward -- you deal massive damage but lose your passive defensives for 5 ticks. Time it carefully, ideally as a finisher when the opponent is low.

---

## 14. Panther

**Role:** Crit / Ambush

*Three strikes from the shadows. Then vanish.*

**Passive -- Shadow Stalk:** Your first 3 attacks deal +30% damage while undetected (you haven't taken damage yet).

**Ability 1 -- Lunge** (Standard, 4.5%): Deal +50% ATK damage and move 2 tiles.

**Ability 2 -- Fade** (Strong, 3.5%): Become "undetected" again for 3 ticks, resetting Shadow Stalk bonus.

**Strategy:** Front-load damage with the Shadow Stalk opener, then use Fade to reset your undetected status and get another burst window. Works well with mixed ATK/SPD builds -- SPD helps you strike first, ATK makes those boosted hits devastating.
