Moreau Island: alignment resilience under pressure

Moreau Island is the next research phase of the project. Moreau Arena measures strategic reasoning under controlled conditions; Moreau Island extends that foundation into a three-zone stress environment where enforcement weakens, information becomes uncertain, and every choice grows more costly. The goal is not just to ask whether a model can optimize, but whether its policy survives pressure.
