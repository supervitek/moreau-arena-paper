Meet the Fighters PREVIEW

Season 1 is a public preview. Tournament data coming soon.

All 14 animals available in Moreau Arena Season 1. Each has a passive (always active), two abilities (proc on any given tick), and a unique playstyle.

Proc tiers: Standard (4.5%) — Base chance to trigger each tick. Modified by WIL (yours boosts it, opponent's resists it). Strong (3.5%) — Lower base chance, but typically more powerful effects.
Bear Burst
When backed into a corner, the Bear doesn't retreat -- it rampages.
Passive — Fury Protocol
When your HP drops below 55%, gain +50% ATK for 4 ticks.
Ability 1 — Berserker Rage Strong 3.5%
Gain +60% ATK for 3 ticks, but lose 40% dodge for the duration.
Ability 2 — Last Stand Strong 3.5%
Survive a killing blow at 1 HP and retaliate with +100% ATK. Single-use per fight.
Strategy

Stack ATK for maximum Fury Protocol damage. Your low-HP zone is your power zone -- the lower you go, the harder you hit. Pair with enough HP to survive reaching that threshold, but don't over-invest in durability or you waste your kit.

Buffalo Tank
Slow to anger, impossible to move.
Passive — Thick Hide
The first hit you take deals 50% reduced damage.
Ability 1 — Thick Hide Block Standard 4.5%
Block ALL damage from 1 hit entirely.
Ability 2 — Iron Will Strong 3.5%
Heal 12% of your max HP. Single-use per fight.
Strategy

Stack HP to maximize the value of Iron Will's percentage-based heal. WIL investment pairs well, giving you regen on top of your innate tankiness. You outlast most opponents but beware sustained damage dealers who ignore your front-loaded defenses.

Boar Bruiser
Lowers its head and doesn't stop.
Passive — Charge
Your first attack deals +85% damage and ignores dodge.
Ability 1 — Stampede Standard 4.5%
Gain +60% ATK and your opponent skips their next attack.
Ability 2 — Gore Strong 3.5%
Deal an attack at -30% ATK, but it ignores dodge entirely.
Strategy

Lead with Charge for a devastating burst opener. Gore punishes dodge-heavy enemies like Fox and Eagle who rely on evasion. Invest in ATK to make your opener and Stampede hits count -- you want fights decided quickly.

Tiger Mobility Burst
Patience. Precision. One perfect strike.
Passive — Ambush Wiring
If your SPD is at least equal to your opponent's SPD, your first attack deals 2x damage. When opponent has Hamstring debuff, gain +15% ATK.
Ability 1 — Pounce Standard 4.5%
Gain +70% ATK and your opponent skips their next attack.
Ability 2 — Hamstring Standard 4.5%
Reduce opponent's SPD by 55% and dodge by 10% for 4 ticks.
Strategy

Invest in SPD to guarantee Ambush Wiring triggers -- a 2x opening hit is massive. Hamstring cripples other fast enemies, stripping their speed advantage. You dominate the early fight; close it out before sustain fighters can recover.

Wolf Sustained
The pack hunts as one. You carry them all.
Passive — Pack Sense
All your ability proc rates gain +1.5% (added to base rates).
Ability 1 — Pack Howl Standard 4.5%
Gain +30% ATK for 4 ticks.
Ability 2 — Rend Standard 4.5%
Apply 3% max HP bleed damage per tick for 3 ticks.
Strategy

WIL investment amplifies both your proc rates and regen, making Wolf the sustained damage king. With Pack Sense boosting all your procs to 6%, you trigger abilities more often than anyone. Play the long game and grind opponents down.

Monkey Wildcard
Chaos is a ladder -- and Monkey climbs fast.
Passive — Primate Cortex
Copy a random ability from your opponent at fight start. All proc rates gain +2.5%.
Ability 1 — Chaos Strike Standard 4.5%
Deal an attack with a random damage multiplier between 1.2x and 2.8x.
Ability 2 — Mimic Strong 3.5%
Copy your opponent's passive at 75% strength.
Strategy

High variance -- your power level depends heavily on what you copy. Monkey is strongest against enemies with powerful passives and abilities (Bear's Fury Protocol at 75% is still devastating). Weakest against simple stat-check fighters with underwhelming passives.

Porcupine Anti-Melee
Hit me. I dare you.
Passive — Quill Armor
50% chance per hit to reflect 15% of melee damage back to attacker.
Ability 1 — Spike Shield Standard 4.5%
Gain +15% reflect damage for 3 ticks (total 30% reflect).
Ability 2 — Curl Up Strong 3.5%
Block the next hit entirely, but you are immobile for 2 ticks.
Strategy

The ultimate counter for ATK stackers -- Bear and Boar hurt themselves hitting you. Invest in HP to survive while they kill themselves on your quills. Beware DOT and anti-heal, which bypass reflect entirely.

Scorpion Anti-Heal
No recovery. No mercy. Just venom.
Passive — Venom Gland
25% chance on hit to reduce target's healing and regen by 50% for 2 ticks.
Ability 1 — Envenom Standard 4.5%
Apply 1.5% max HP poison DOT for 3 ticks. Refreshes on reapplication, never stacks.
Ability 2 — Tail Strike Strong 3.5%
Deal +80% ATK damage, ignoring 50% of the target's damage reduction.
Strategy

Hard counter to WIL-heavy regen builds and Buffalo's tankiness. Venom Gland halves all healing, and Tail Strike pierces damage reduction. Struggles against fast burst fighters like Tiger and Eagle who can kill you before your debuffs matter.

Vulture Finisher
Patient. Circling. Waiting for weakness.
Passive — Carrion Feeder
Gain +3.5% ATK for every 10% HP your opponent is missing.
Ability 1 — Death Spiral Standard 4.5%
Gain +40% ATK for 2 ticks if opponent is below 50% HP.
Ability 2 — Feast Strong 3.5%
Heal 30% of damage dealt this tick.
Strategy

You get stronger as fights progress -- a nearly dead opponent gives you up to +10% ATK from Carrion Feeder alone, plus Death Spiral on top. Pair with WIL for sustain to outlast opponents, then finish with massive scaling damage. Avoid burst fighters who kill you before you ramp up.

Rhino Anti-Burst Tank
You can hit hard. But you can't hit hard enough.
Passive — Bulwark Frame
When HP drops below 60%, gain 28% damage reduction for 4 ticks (one-time trigger).
Ability 1 — Horn Charge Standard 4.5%
Deal +60% ATK damage.
Ability 2 — Fortify Strong 3.5%
Gain +30% damage reduction for 4 ticks.
Strategy

Stack HP to maximize the Bulwark threshold -- the higher your max HP, the bigger the hit needs to be before reduction kicks in. Hard counter to Bear and Tiger burst. Weak against sustained DOT damage that comes in small ticks, bypassing Bulwark entirely.

Viper DOT
A slow death is still a death.
Passive — Hemotoxin
12% chance per hit to apply 1% max HP DOT for 2 ticks. Refreshes on reapplication, never stacks.
Ability 1 — Toxic Bite Standard 4.5%
Apply 1.5% max HP DOT for 4 ticks (refresh only).
Ability 2 — Shed Skin Strong 3.5%
Remove all your debuffs and heal 8% max HP.
Strategy

Consistent ticking damage that wears down tanks over time. Your DOT bypasses Rhino's Bulwark Frame since it deals small per-tick damage. Countered hard by Scorpion's anti-heal, which cuts your DOT healing and sustain in half.

Fox Anti-Proc
You can't hit what you can't predict.
Passive — Cunning
Your opponent's proc rates are reduced by 50%.
Ability 1 — Outfox Standard 4.5%
Dodge the next 3 attacks entirely.
Ability 2 — Trick Strong 3.5%
Steal your opponent's next proc -- you get the effect instead of them.
Strategy

Shuts down proc-reliant fighters like Wolf and Monkey. High SPD combined with Cunning makes you incredibly evasive and disruptive. Struggles against raw ATK fighters like Bear and Boar who don't need procs to deal damage.

Eagle Kite
You fight on the ground. Eagle fights from the sky.
Passive — Aerial Advantage
While your HP is above 60%, gain +15% dodge and +1 movement range.
Ability 1 — Dive Strong 3.5%
Deal +100% ATK in a single devastating strike, but lose Aerial Advantage for 5 ticks.
Ability 2 — Tailwind Standard 4.5%
Gain +10% dodge for 3 ticks.
Strategy

Kite with superior movement and build SPD for maximum evasion. Dive is high-risk, high-reward -- you deal massive damage but lose your passive defensives for 5 ticks. Time it carefully, ideally as a finisher when the opponent is low.

Panther Crit / Ambush
Three strikes from the shadows. Then vanish.
Passive — Shadow Stalk
Your first 3 attacks deal +35% damage while undetected. Taking damage does not break stealth.
Ability 1 — Lunge Standard 4.5%
Deal +70% ATK damage.
Ability 2 — Fade Strong 3.5%
Become "undetected" again for 3 ticks, resetting Shadow Stalk bonus.
Strategy

Front-load damage with the Shadow Stalk opener, then use Fade to reset your undetected status and get another burst window. Works well with mixed ATK/SPD builds -- SPD helps you strike first, ATK makes those boosted hits devastating.
