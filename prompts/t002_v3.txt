Your task: design a combatant for Moreau Arena, a 1-versus-1 grid combat game (8×8 board).

CONSTRAINTS:
- You have a budget of exactly 20 stat points, split across HP, ATK, SPD, and WIL (each must be at least 1)
- Pick one of the six animals below — each comes with a passive and two triggered abilities

HOW STATS WORK:
  max_hp = 50 + 10 * HP
    (HP=3 means 80 health, HP=8 means 130 health, HP=14 means 190 health)
  base_dmg = floor(2 + 0.85 * ATK)
    (ATK=8 means 8 damage, ATK=14 means 13 damage)
  dodge = max(0%, min(30%, 2.5% * (SPD - 1)))
    Caveat: SPD=1 yields 0% dodge; SPD=5 yields 10%; SPD=13 hits the 30% ceiling
  ability_resist = min(60%, WIL * 3.3%)
    This is your chance to negate the ENEMY's ability procs. WIL=1 → 3.3%; WIL=10 → 33%; maximum 60%
  ability_proc_bonus = WIL * 0.08% (stacks onto YOUR base proc rates)
    With WIL=8, all your abilities gain +0.64% proc chance

HOW COMBAT WORKS:
- The fight runs on a tick system, capped at 60 ticks
- At tick 30 a contracting ring appears, dealing growing damage to anyone on the outer squares
- Abilities fire randomly each tick based on (base proc rate + WIL bonus)
- Each tick resolves in this order: Attack → Buff ticks → Ability procs → Fury check → DoTs → Ring → Second Wind → Regen
- Abilities with 3.5% base rate: Berserker Rage, Last Stand, Gore, Mimic, Iron Will
- Abilities with 4.5% base rate: Pounce, Hamstring, Pack Howl, Rend, Chaos Strike, Stampede, Thick Hide

THE SIX ANIMALS:
  BEAR
    Passive: Fury Protocol — gains bonus attack power when taking damage. Below 50% HP, Fury kicks in: +3 base_dmg for 5 ticks.
    Abilities:
  - Berserker Rage: Temporary ATK increase (+4 base_dmg), lasts 3 ticks. Triggers at 3.5% per tick.
  - Last Stand: 2.0× damage multiplier when health falls below 15%. Single use per fight. Triggers at 3.5%.

  BUFFALO
    Passive: Thick Hide — natural damage absorption. Every incoming hit is reduced by a flat 2 damage.
    Abilities:
  - Thick Hide: Blocks 3 additional damage for 1 tick. Triggers at 4.5% per tick.
  - Iron Will: Cheats death once, surviving at 1 HP instead of dying. Single use per fight. Triggers at 3.5%.

  BOAR
    Passive: Charge — powerful opening blow. The very first attack deals 1.5× base_dmg.
    Abilities:
  - Stampede: Hits an area for 0.4× base_dmg and pushes the target back. Triggers at 4.5% per tick.
  - Gore: Strikes for 0.60× damage, but the hit cannot be dodged. Triggers at 3.5% per tick.

  TIGER
    Passive: Ambush Wiring — evasion specialist with a guaranteed opener. Gains +5% dodge; first attack bypasses the opponent's dodge.
    Abilities:
  - Pounce: +70% damage and stuns the enemy (they miss their next attack). Triggers at 4.5% per tick.
  - Hamstring: Slashes opponent's SPD by 55% and dodge by 10% for 4 ticks. Triggers at 4.5% per tick.

  WOLF
    Passive: Pack Sense — enhanced ability triggering. All ability proc rates gain +1.5% bonus.
    Abilities:
  - Pack Howl: Buffs ATK (+3 base_dmg) for 4 ticks. Triggers at 4.5% per tick.
  - Rend: Inflicts a bleed: 3 damage per tick for 3 ticks. Triggers at 4.5% per tick.

  MONKEY
    Passive: Primate Cortex — learns and copies the opponent's tricks via Mimic.
    Abilities:
  - Chaos Strike: Multiplies attack damage by a random factor between 0.5× and 2.0×. Triggers at 4.5% per tick.
  - Mimic: Steals the opponent's last triggered ability. Triggers at 3.5% per tick.
